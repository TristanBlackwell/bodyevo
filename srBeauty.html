Hello, my name is Sophie, I'm 20 years old. I am a self employed, fully qualified beautician and massage therapist, offering a wide range of treatments to the public and currently based in Witney.
I have always been driven to work for myself since school and being surrounded by passionate family and friends who have done the same thing has driven me to get to where I am today! Anything can happen, if you want it too.
I LOVE making people feel good in themselves. I have always had a big heart, wanting to help people as much as possible. I am a massive believer in ‘treat others how you would like to be treated’ and being in the beauty industry really helps you help others in a completely deeper, intimate level, which is amazing. You form some great connections and learn a lot.
